"I am going to be the CEO of Google."

     This is what I told my resident hall director when I met her for the first time in the first few days of my freshman year of college. I would very much love to be the CEO of Google or, at least, my own version of Google. A successful software company that will allow me to leave my mark on the world. I have always been very ambitious and strive to be the best at what I do.
     My name is Ahsan Qureshi and I am a software developer. I fell in love with computer science in high school when I took my very first computer science class and learned my first programming language, Dr. Racket. I dropped pre-med in my last semester of senior year to fully pursue my dream.

You can follow me on my journey as I work to better the world and make life easier for everyone around me. Check out the "Projects" tab to see what I have worked on or am currently working on!
